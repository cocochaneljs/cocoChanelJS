Welcome to cocoChanelJS.

Hey! I am a programmer, who once thought: "What if we don't need to use Photoshop for websites design? Why all the tools that are for web-designers are built on a renderer that's simulating pages and not actually rendering them? Why tools like that need to cost a ton of money?"

I found the solution: "Hey, I am a programmer , why don't I take this flaw and transform it into an opportunity to make every web-desinger's life easier?" So I started to build this tool, it's easy to use, actively maintained (all on my free time), it's in HTML that builds HTML, no need to install, just download open with your browser the file: "index.html" and voila!

Try stuff out, for the moment there's no tutorial on how to use it but it's mostly straight forward:

  1. Select your element.

  2. Chose an action.

  3. Voila!

Please use Google Chrome (not yet tested in other browsers)
